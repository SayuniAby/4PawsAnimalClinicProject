Welcome To 4Paws Animal Clinic

We are Sri Lanka's foremost full-service veterinary hospital

          4Paws Animal Clinic, established on August 20, 2021, in Rathnapura, Sri Lanka, has become a beacon of hope and a trusted partner for pet owners.
   Dr. Damindu Wickramasinghe and Dr. Ushan Nirmal, the visionary founders, have created a haven for pets, offering comprehensive veterinary care and a commitment to animal welfare. 
   As they continue to grow and expand their services, their impact on the community is sure to be felt for many more years to come, making Rathnapura a better place for pets and their loving owners.
