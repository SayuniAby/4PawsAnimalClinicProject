Birds

Birds are vibrant and intelligent creatures, often forming strong bonds with their owners. Their singing, playful nature, and ability to learn tricks make them delightful pets.

How To Care For Your Bird

1. Provide a spacious cage with clean perches.

2. Feed a balanced diet of pellets, fresh fruits, and seeds.

3. Regular interaction helps keep birds social and happy.

4. Offer bathing or misting to maintain feather health.

5. Ensure routine veterinary visits for health checks.

How To Identify If a Bird Has a Disease

Common signs of illness in birds include:

1. Fluffed-up feathers or weakness

2. Labored breathing or nasal discharge

3. Loss of appetite or drastic weight changes

4. Changes in droppings or feather loss

5. Lethargy or abnormal behavior

If you notice any of these symptoms, consult a veterinarian immediately. Early detection can prevent complications and ensure timely treatment.
