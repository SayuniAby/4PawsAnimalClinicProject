About Us

At 4Paws, we believe in a world where pets are cherished as family.

Founded on August 20, 2021, in Rathnapura, Sri Lanka, 4Paws Animal Clinic has quickly become a sanctuary for pets and a trusted ally for their owners. Under the visionary leadership of Dr. Damindu Wickramasinghe and Dr. Ushan Nirmal, we are committed to delivering compassionate veterinary care that goes beyond treatment—providing comfort, healing, and lifelong support for your furry companions.

Our Philosophy

Vision
To be the leading veterinary clinic in Sri Lanka,
providing compassionate, innovative, and accessible pet care that 
strengthens the bond between pets and their families, 
ensuring a healthier and happier future for every animal.
Mission
At 4Paws, we are committed to delivering exceptional 
veterinary services with expertise and care. 
Through advanced medical treatments, personalized wellness plans, 
and a deep dedication to animal welfare, we aim to support 
pet owners in providing the best life for their beloved companions. 
We strive to create a welcoming environment where every pet receives 
the love and attention they deserve—because pets are family.

Our Core Values

Compassion
We treat every pet with the same love and care as our own. 
Our team is dedicated to ensuring animals receive the kindness and 
respect they deserve.
Integrity
We uphold honesty and transparency in all our services. 
Pet owners can trust us to provide ethical and professional veterinary 
care at all times.
Excellence
We strive for the highest standards in veterinary treatment, 
using advanced technology and expert knowledge to provide top-tier
healthcare for pets.
Commitment to Animal Welfare
Beyond just treatment, we advocate for the well-being of all animals, 
supporting responsible pet ownership and better lives for pets in our community.
Personalized Care
Every pet is unique, and so is our approach. 
We tailor treatments to each animal’s specific needs, ensuring comfort
and optimal health.
Teamwork & Community
We collaborate with pet owners, fellow veterinarians, 
and the wider community to create a network of support for animal care.
